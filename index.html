The Staff Hacks

In October 2015, someone discovered that Minecraft stores session IDs in the logs file, every time you start the game. That means, if someone sends you their log file, you could log into their account.

Because of the time zone differences it was hard to set up, but we managed to get Necrai and Mj to message every moderator and try to convince them into sending their logs.

Guess what? A moderator did send their log file. Necrai and Mj unbanned a few network banned people, etc, then we casually went and played bridges from my freshly unbanned account, as if nothing happened.




https://youtu.be/_EK6G08Ikxs
